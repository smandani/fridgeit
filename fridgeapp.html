How It Works

SCAN

A camera scans and records the barcode of your food item.

STORE

The app stores your food item information and provides a detailed report about expiration dates and dietary notices.

SAVE

Since the app keeps that information about your food items, it lets you know about when your food is about to expire, helping you save time and money!
